Welcome to sfDB

The San Francisco Database is a catalogue of planned new construction in San Francisco. It includes new residential, office, infrastructure, and parks, and attractions in the area.

sfDB was put together to better understand the recent changes in San Francisco.

This site uses these technologies: HTML5, CSS3, SASS/SCSS, AngularJS + libraries, Angular Google Maps, Bootstrap, and Gulp. In addition to these, I can also work with jQuery, plain JavaScript, and Wordpress. Contact me if you would like to know more about sfDB.
